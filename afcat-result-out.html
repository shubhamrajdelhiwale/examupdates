How to Calculate AFCAT Marks?

To calculate your AFCAT marks, candidates must understand the official marking scheme used in the examination. The AFCAT exam contains 100 questions. Each correct answer carries 3 marks while 1 mark is deducted for every incorrect answer.

  1. Count the total number of questions answered correctly.
  2. Multiply the number of correct answers by 3.
  3. Count how many questions you answered incorrectly.
  4. Multiply the number of incorrect answers by 1.
  5. Subtract the negative marks from the total marks obtained for correct answers.

AFCAT 1 Cut Off 2026

The AFCAT Cut Off 2026 has been released along with the result announcement. Candidates must score equal to or higher than the minimum qualifying marks to move forward in the selection process.

The cut-off marks vary every year depending on the exam difficulty level and the number of vacancies available. Separate cut-off marks are announced for AFCAT 1 and Engineering Knowledge Test (EKT).

What After AFCAT 1 Result 2026?

After the declaration of the AFCAT result, candidates who qualify the written examination will be called for the Air Force Selection Board (AFSB) tests. These tests include psychological tests, group activities and personal interviews.

Candidates clearing the AFSB stage must also undergo a medical examination. The final merit list is prepared based on AFCAT marks, AFSB performance and medical fitness. Selected candidates will then undergo training at the Air Force Academy.
