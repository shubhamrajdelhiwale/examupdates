All About Bihar TRE-4

The Bihar Teacher Recruitment Examination (TRE-4) is a recruitment drive by BSEB to hire qualified teachers for Primary, Middle, Secondary and Higher Secondary schools. Candidates must meet educational & certificate requirements (D.El.Ed / B.Ed and valid TET/STET/CTET where applicable).

TRE-4 evaluates subject knowledge, teaching aptitude and classroom pedagogy. Aspirants should focus on NCERT basics, subject-depth and pedagogy to perform well.

Exam Pattern & Syllabus (Summary)

Primary (1–5)

  • Child Development & Pedagogy
  • Language I (Hindi/Urdu)
  • Language II (English)
  • Mathematics & EVS

Middle (6–8)

  • Pedagogy & Teaching Methodologies
  • Language I & II
  • Subject-wise (Math/Science/SST)

Secondary (9–10)

  • Subject Knowledge (Maths, Science, SST, Languages)
  • Teaching Aptitude & Reasoning
  • General Awareness (State + National)

Higher Secondary (11–12)

  • Subject Expertise (Science/Arts/Commerce/Vocational)
  • Pedagogy & Instructional Techniques

Eligibility

  • Indian citizen (Bihar domicile rules may apply for certain posts)
  • D.El.Ed / B.Ed (as per post)
  • Valid TET/CTET/STET certificate where required
  • Age: typically 21–37 years (relaxations applicable)

Vacancy & Apply

TRE-4 expects a large vacancy pool. See the official notification for post-wise vacancy counts and category-wise allocation.
