CBSE Class 10 Passing Marks 2026

To pass CBSE Class 10, students need at least 33% marks in each subject. This includes both theory (80 marks) and internal assessment or practical (20 marks). Students usually need to pass by combining both theory and internal marks, not separately.

The CBSE mark sheet will include details such as the student’s name, father’s and mother’s names, school name, subject names, roll number, board name, marks obtained, grades, and remarks.

CBSE Class 10 Results 2026: How to Check?

Follow the steps below to check and download the CBSE Class 10 result 2026 online:

  • Go to the official CBSE result website cbse.nic.in
  • Click on the ‘Secondary School Examination Results 2026’ link
  • Enter your roll number, school number, admit card ID, and date of birth
  • The CBSE 10th result will appear on the screen
  • Check subject-wise marks and download the marksheet
  • Take a printout for future reference
