How to Calculate Exam Score Through CTET Answer Key 2026?

To calculate your CTET score, it is important to compare your marked responses with the official CTET Answer Key 2026. By checking correct and incorrect responses, candidates can estimate their probable score before the result is announced. As per the official marking scheme, candidates are awarded 1 mark for each correct answer and no negative marking is applied for wrong or unattempted questions.

1

Start matching the answers given in the answer key with the responses you marked in the OMR sheet during the exam.

2

Add +1 mark for every correct answer. Since there is no negative marking, do not deduct marks for incorrect or unattempted questions.

3

Repeat the process for all questions to calculate your total estimated score. Double-check your calculation to avoid any miscalculation.
