Things to Carry at the Exam Centre

Candidates must reach the exam centre well in time with the following documents for identity verification and smooth entry procedures.

  • A clear printed copy of the DSSSB MTS admit card
  • A valid photo identity proof such as PAN Card, Voter ID Card, Driving Licence, Passport or Aadhaar
  • A recent colour photograph matching the one uploaded in the application form
  • Any additional material permitted in the admit card instructions (for example pen or pencil)

Things Not Allowed at Exam Centre 2026

DSSSB has clearly listed banned items. Carrying prohibited items may result in cancellation of candidature. Candidates should bring only essential documents.

  • Mobile phones or smartphones
  • Study material, notes, or papers
  • Calculators, laptops, or tablets
  • Smartwatches or electronic watches
  • Bluetooth devices, earbuds, or headphones
  • Cameras or recording devices
  • Any metallic or electronic gadgets

What If You Find an Error on Your Admit Card?

If there is any discrepancy in your name, date of birth, or photograph on the DSSSB Assistant Teacher Admit Card 2026, you must contact the board immediately.

Candidates can reach out to the DSSSB helpdesk via the official website or visit the board’s office in Karkardooma, Delhi, with supporting documents to get the errors rectified before the exam date.

DSSSB PRT Exam Day Dress Code

DSSSB is very strict regarding its dress code to prevent high-tech cheating. Failure to follow these guidelines may result in denial of entry into the examination hall.

  • Footwear: Shoes are strictly prohibited. Candidates must wear slippers or sandals with low heels.
  • Clothing: Wear light clothes with half-sleeves only. Long sleeves are not allowed.
  • Buttons & Accessories: Avoid clothes with big buttons, brooches, badges, or flowers.
  • Jewelry: All ornaments including rings, earrings, nose-pins, chains, necklaces, pendants, and even hair bands/clips must be removed before entry.
