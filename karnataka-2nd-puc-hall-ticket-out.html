How to Download 2nd PUC Hall Ticket 2026 PDF?

The hall ticket has been issued in the login window. Candidates appearing in the exam can follow the step-by-step guide below to download the 2nd PUC hall ticket.

  • Visit the official website of the Department of Pre-University Education, Karnataka at kseab.karnataka.gov.in.
  • Click on the announcements tab and search for the hall ticket link.
  • After clicking on the direct link, the login window will appear on the screen.
  • Enter the registration number in the required fields.
  • Carefully verify all the details mentioned on the admit card.
  • Download the 2nd PUC Hall Ticket 2026 PDF and keep it safe for future reference.

What is the Process of kseab.karnataka.gov.in Login?

Candidates can refer to the steps provided below to access the hall ticket PDF from the login window.

  • Visit the official website at kseab.karnataka.gov.in.
  • Click on the “PUC Corner” tab from the homepage.
  • In the login window, enter the registration number in the required field.
  • Solve the captcha and enter the displayed result.
  • Click on the login tab to access and download the hall ticket.

Applicants are required to follow all the instructions mentioned on the admit card. Students must strictly adhere to the exam day guidelines at the Karnataka 2nd PUC examination centre.

2nd PUC Hall Ticket FAQs

What details are required to download the 2nd PUC hall ticket?

Applicants need to enter their school registration number in the official login window to download the Karnataka 2nd PUC hall ticket 2026.

What is the Karnataka 2nd PUC exam date 2026?

The Karnataka 2nd PUC exams 2026 are scheduled to be conducted from 28th February to 17th March 2026 in offline pen-and-paper mode.

What documents are required at the exam centre?

Students must carry their printed hall ticket along with a valid school ID card to the examination centre.

Can I show my 2nd PUC admit card on my mobile phone?

No, candidates are required to carry a hard copy of the admit card. Digital copies on mobile phones are not accepted inside the exam hall.

How many attempts are allowed for 2nd PUC Karnataka?

The Karnataka 2nd PUC board conducts three examinations annually (Exam 1, Exam 2, and Exam 3). Students can attempt the exam up to three times in an academic year, and the best score among the attempts is considered.
