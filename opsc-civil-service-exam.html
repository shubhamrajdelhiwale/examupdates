About OPSC Civil Service Exam 2025

The Odisha Public Service Commission (OPSC) released the OPSC Civil Services Notification 2025 on 3 January 2026 on its official website. As per the official schedule, the online application process was conducted from 20 January 2026 to 20 February 2026. A total of 465 vacancies have been announced for recruitment through the OPSC Civil Services Examination. The OPSC Civil Services Preliminary Examination 2025 is scheduled to be held on 7 June 2026. The examination is conducted to recruit eligible candidates for various Group A and Group B posts under the Government of Odisha.

The Odisha Public Service Commission conducts the Civil Services Examination for entry-level appointments to different administrative services under the Odisha Civil Services framework. The selection process consists of three stages – Preliminary Examination, Main Examination, and Personality Test (Interview). Candidates must qualify at each stage to proceed to the next phase of the selection process. Final recruitment is based on performance in the Mains Examination and Personality Test.

OPSC OCS 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Citizenship

A candidate must be a citizen of India.

Age Limit

A candidate must have attained the age of 21 years and must not be above 42 years as on 1st January 2025 (i.e., born not earlier than 2nd January 1983 and not later than 1st January 2004).

  • Upper age limit is relaxable by 5 years for SC/ST/SEBC/Women/Ex-Servicemen.
  • 10 years relaxation for PwD (40% or more disability).
  • Cumulative benefit allowed for PwD of SC/ST/SEBC categories.

Educational Qualification (Essential)

A candidate must hold a Bachelor’s Degree from a University incorporated by an Act of the Central or State Legislature in India or an institution established under Section-3 of the UGC Act, 1956, or a Foreign University approved by the Central Government.

Odia Language Requirement

The candidate must be able to read, write, and speak Odia and must have:

  • Passed Middle School Examination with Odia as a language subject, OR
  • Passed HSC or equivalent with Odia as medium or subject, OR
  • Passed Odia as a language subject in Class VII final examination, OR
  • Passed a test in Odia conducted by the Government of Odisha.

Marital Status Condition

A candidate having more than one spouse living (or in case of a woman candidate, married to a person having one spouse living) shall not be eligible unless exempted by the State Government.

Government Servants

Government servants (temporary or permanent) are eligible, provided they possess requisite qualifications, fall within the prescribed age limit, and obtain a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the competent authority.

Character & Health

A candidate must have good moral character and be of sound mental and physical health, free from any physical defect that may interfere with the discharge of official duties.

OPSC OCS 2026 – General Information / Instructions

  • The provisions of the Odisha Conduct of Examinations Act, 1988 are applicable; any malpractice may attract disciplinary action and criminal prosecution.
  • Penalty provisions under Rule-18 of Odisha Civil Services Combined Competitive Recruitment Examination Rules, 1991 shall apply.
  • Reservation and relaxation benefits for SC, ST and SEBC are admissible only to candidates of Odisha.
  • Community/caste status mentioned in the online application will be treated as final and cannot be changed later.
  • The number of vacancies is subject to change depending on public service requirements.
  • Misrepresentation or suppression of information will result in cancellation of candidature.
  • Mere empanelment does not confer any right to appointment unless approved by the Government.
  • No request for withdrawal of candidature or change of category will be entertained.
  • Service/post preference once submitted cannot be changed later.
  • Answers must be written in the candidate’s own handwriting; PwD candidates may use a scribe as per rules.

How to Apply for OPSC OCS 2026

  • Read the detailed advertisement available on the official OPSC website before applying.
  • Apply online only through the official website: https://opsc.gov.in.
  • The registration and fee payment window is open from 20 January 2026 to 20 February 2026 (5:00 PM).
  • The last date for submission of registered online application is 27 February 2026 (5:00 PM).
  • Applications submitted through any mode other than online will not be accepted.
  • After the Main Written Exam result, shortlisted candidates must submit a hard copy of the online application along with required documents during document verification.

OPSC OCS 2026 Answer Key

Preliminary Exam

Odisha Public Service Commission releases the answer key of the OPSC preliminary examination after completion of the exam. Candidates can verify their marked responses with the officially published OPSC answer key. The preliminary exam consists of two papers – General Studies Paper I and General Studies Paper II – each carrying 200 marks.

Main Exam

The OPSC will release the answer key for the main examination after the completion of the mains exam. The OPSC Main exam consists of compulsory and optional papers carrying 1400 marks and 1200 marks respectively. Candidates can cross-check their answers using the official answer key provided by the commission.

OPSC OCS 2026 Result

Preliminary Exam Result

The Commission declares the result of each stage separately. Candidates qualifying in the preliminary exam become eligible for the main examination. The preliminary scores are not counted for the final merit. The final ranking is determined based on performance in the mains exam and interview.

Main Exam Result

The OPSC Main result is published online on the official website. Qualified candidates receive a scorecard and are called for the personality test/interview. Final merit is prepared based on cumulative marks obtained in the mains examination and interview.

Documents Required at OPSC Civil Services Counselling

  • ✔ Marksheet and Certificate of Class X
  • ✔ Marksheet and Certificate of Class XII
  • ✔ Scorecard of Preliminary Examination
  • ✔ Hardcopy of Online Application Form

OPSC OCS 2026 Selection Process

The selection process consists of three stages:

  • Preliminary Examination
  • Main Examination
  • Personality Test / Interview

Interview / Personality Test

After declaration of the OPSC Main examination result, shortlisted candidates appear for the Personality Test. This round evaluates intellectual capability, mental alertness, social traits, and awareness of current affairs. The interview carries 250 marks, and final merit is prepared based on cumulative marks obtained in mains and interview.

❓ OPSC OCS 2026 – Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How many vacancies are announced in OPSC OCS 2026? ⌄

A total of 465 vacancies have been announced under the Odisha Civil Services Examination. These include Group A and Group B services as per the official notification.

Who conducts the OPSC Civil Services Examination? ⌄

The examination is conducted by the Odisha Public Service Commission (OPSC) to recruit candidates for various Group A and Group B posts under the Odisha Government.

What is the age limit for OPSC OCS 2026? ⌄

Candidates must be between 21 and 42 years as on 1st January 2025. Age relaxation is applicable for SC/ST/SEBC/Women/Ex-Servicemen and PwD categories as per government rules.

What is the salary of OPSC Group A and Group B officers? ⌄

Group A officers receive pay at Level-12 (₹56,100/-), while Group B officers are placed in Level-10 (₹44,900/-) under the ORSP Rules, 2017, along with admissible allowances.

What is the educational qualification required? ⌄

Candidates must possess a Bachelor’s Degree from a recognized university established by an Act of Parliament or State Legislature or deemed university under UGC norms.

How many attempts are allowed in OPSC OCS? ⌄

General category candidates can attempt the exam up to 6 times. SEBC candidates are allowed 9 attempts, while there is no attempt limit for SC/ST candidates as per rules.

What is the selection process of OPSC Civil Services? ⌄

The selection process includes: Preliminary Examination, Main Examination, and Personality Test (Interview). Final merit is prepared based on marks obtained in mains and interview.
