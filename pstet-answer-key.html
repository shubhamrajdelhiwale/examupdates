How to Download PSTET 2026 Answer Key PDF?

Downloading the PSTET Answer Key 2026 is a simple process through the official website. Candidates can save or print the answer key to calculate their expected marks and compare responses with the official answers. Follow the steps below to download the PSTET answer key PDF.

  • Visit Official Website: Open the official website pstet2025.org or the PSEB official portal in your browser.
  • Locate Answer Key Section: On the homepage, click on the Answer Key link to proceed to the relevant page.
  • Select Your Paper: Choose Paper 1 or Paper 2 and select the respective question booklet set (A, B, C, or D).
  • Download PDF: Open the answer key PDF and download it to your device for easy access.
  • Verify Answers: Carefully compare your answers with the official answer key provided in the document.
  • Save or Print: Print or save the PDF for future reference and score calculation.

PSTET Answer Key Objection Process

If candidates find any discrepancy in the PSTET provisional answer key, they can challenge it through the official objection portal. The objection window remains open for a limited time after the answer key release. Follow the steps below to submit your objection.

  • Login: Visit the official website and log in using your registered credentials.
  • Objection Form: Click on the Objection Form or challenge link available on the dashboard.
  • Identify the Question: Select the question number and mention the correct option according to your understanding.
  • Attach Supporting Documents: Upload valid references such as textbooks or academic material supporting your objection.
  • Payment & Submission: Pay the required fee per objection (if applicable) and submit the challenge before the deadline.

After reviewing all the objections, the board will publish the final PSTET Answer Key 2026, which will be used to prepare the final PSTET result.

How to Calculate Scores Using the PSTET Answer Key 2026

Candidates can estimate their expected score using the PSTET Answer Key 2026. By comparing their responses with the official answers, candidates can calculate probable marks according to the official marking scheme.

  • Correct Answer: +1 Mark
  • Incorrect Answer: 0 Mark (No Negative Marking)

  • Formula: Total Score = (Number of Correct Responses) × 1
