RBI Assistant 2026 Selection Process

RBI Assistant Selection Process 2026 consists of three stages to ensure a comprehensive evaluation of candidates. The stages include Preliminary Exam, Mains Exam, and Language Proficiency Test. Each stage evaluates reasoning ability, numerical skills, and language proficiency to ensure fair selection.

Preliminary Examination

The preliminary exam consists of 100 objective-type questions for 100 marks to be completed in 60 minutes. Sections include English Language, Numerical Ability, and Reasoning Ability. Candidates must secure sectional cutoff marks to qualify for the Mains examination.

Mains Examination

The Mains exam consists of 200 objective-type questions for 200 marks to be completed in 135 minutes. Sections include Reasoning, English Language, Numerical Ability, General Knowledge, and Computer Knowledge. Candidates must secure sectional cutoff marks and minimum qualifying marks to move to the next stage.

Language Proficiency Test

Candidates qualifying the Mains exam will appear for the Language Proficiency Test (LPT), conducted in the local/official language of the state applied for. This is a qualifying test to assess language proficiency. Candidates must qualify this stage for final selection.
