RRC WR Apprentice Recruitment 2026: Apply Online for 5349 Posts at rrc-wr.com

By FreeMockTestAdda Desk | 2 min read

The Railway Recruitment Cell (RRC), Western Railway (WR) has released the RRC WR Apprentice Recruitment 2026 notification for 5349 apprentice posts. Eligible candidates can apply online through the official website rrc-wr.com. The recruitment drive aims to fill various trade apprentice vacancies across different divisions of Western Railway.

Short Information

Western Railway Apprentice Recruitment 2026 has been officially announced by Railway Recruitment Cell (RRC WR) under Notification No. RRC/WR/04/2025. A total of 5349 Act Apprentice posts are available across various trades such as Fitter, Electrician, Welder, Carpenter and others for the training year 2025‚Äì26.

The online application process will begin on 21 February 2026 and the last date to apply is 23 March 2026. Eligible candidates must submit their application form through the official website www.rrc-wr.com.

Applicants must have passed 10th with minimum 50% marks along with a valid ITI certificate in the relevant trade from NCVT/SCVT. The age limit is 15 to 24 years as on 23 March 2026, with age relaxation applicable for reserved categories as per government norms.

The selection process will be purely merit-based, considering marks obtained in 10th and ITI. There will be no written examination or interview. Selected candidates will receive stipend as per Railway Apprenticeship Rules during the training period.
