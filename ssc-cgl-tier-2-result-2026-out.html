How to Check SSC CGL Final Result 2025-26?

The SSC CGL Result 2026 is now released on the official SSC website. The result will show your marks, qualification status, and merit list.

It is recommended to download and save a copy of the result for future use, as it will be needed during document verification and final selection.

Check the steps to download SSC CGL Result PDF below:

Step 1: Click on the direct link provided above or visit the official website of SSC

Step 2: On the top right portion of the screen click on results which will take you to the results section

Step 3: In the results section select CGL

Step 4: Now find the SSC CGL Result PDF file and click on Download

Step 5: Open the file and check whether your Name and Roll no. is there on the list

What Next After SSC CGL Result 2026 Tier 2?

After the SSC CGL Tier 2 Exam 2026, candidates will be shortlisted based on their performance in the exam. The Tier 2 exam tests Quantitative Ability, Reasoning, English, and General Awareness.

The final selection will be made on the basis of total marks scored in Tier 2 only. Candidates who qualify will be called for document verification.

Finally, a category-wise merit list will be prepared based on exam marks and post preferences filled during the application process.
