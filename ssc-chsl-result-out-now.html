SSC CHSL Result 2025-26 Tier 1 Out @ssc.gov.in, Cut Off, Result Direct Download Link

27 Feb, 2026 — MockTest Adda

The SSC CHSL Result 2025-26 for Tier 1 has been officially declared on 27 February 2026 by the Staff Selection Commission at the official website ssc.gov.in. The Tier 1 examination was conducted from 12 to 30 November 2025 to fill a total of 3,131 vacancies across various central government departments.

The recruitment drive is being held for posts including Lower Division Clerk (LDC), Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA), and Data Entry Operator (DEO). Candidates who appeared in the examination can now check their qualifying status, category-wise cut off marks, and details regarding the Tier 2 examination.

Applicants are advised to download the result PDF from the official website and search their roll number in the list of shortlisted candidates. The next stage of the selection process will include the Tier 2 exam as per the SSC schedule. Candidates must regularly check the official portal for further updates regarding admit cards and exam dates.
