SSC GD Exam Postponed; Check Official Notice Here

SSC announces deferment of CAPFs, SSF and Assam Rifles GD exam scheduled from February 23; fresh dates to be announced later.The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has decided to postpone the SSC Constable (GD) in Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs) and SSF, and Rifleman (GD) in Assam Rifles Examination 2026. The announcement was made by SSC today (February 17). While the government exam has been deferred without stating the reason, a fresh exam date has not been announced. SSC has mentioned that fresh exam dates will be announced later. As per the previous exam dates, the SSC GD exam was scheduled to be conducted on February 23.

Feb 17, 2026 | By Shubham Raj | 3 min read

SSC GD Exam New Date 2026

"The Computer Based Examination for Constable (GD) in Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs) and SSF, and Rifleman (GD) in Assam Rifles Examination, 2026, which was scheduled from 23rd February, 2026 onwards in terms of Commission's Notice HQ-EC033/7/2025-EC dated 02.01.2026, is hereby deferred till further notice. Fresh dates will be announced in due course," SSC stated in an official notice.

SSC is also expected to activate the slot booking window on its official website, allowing candidates to check their exam date, shift timing, and examination city.

SSC GD Constable Exam Pattern & Selection Process

The first stage of the recruitment process is a Computer-Based Test (CBT) conducted to shortlist candidates for physical tests. The exam consists of 80 multiple-choice questions, each carrying 2 marks, making a total of 160 marks. For each incorrect answer, 0.25 marks will be deducted. The duration of the exam is 60 minutes (1 hour).

Subjects Included in SSC GD CBT

  • General Intelligence & Reasoning – 20 Questions
  • General Knowledge & General Awareness – 20 Questions
  • Elementary Mathematics – 20 Questions
  • English / Hindi Language – 20 Questions

Minimum Qualifying Marks

  • UR – 30%
  • OBC / EWS – 25%
  • All Other Categories – 20%

After qualifying in the CBT stage, candidates must appear for the Physical Efficiency Test (PET) and Physical Standard Test (PST). Finally shortlisted candidates will undergo medical examination before final selection.

SSC GD Constable Exam Slot Booking

The slot booking will be done online through login on the SSC website. Candidates must complete the slot booking process within the given time. If candidates fail to book slot on time, they will get exam city, shift and date as per availability. Hence, to avoid inconvenience, candidates must carefully complete this process.

How to Complete SSC GD Self Slot Booking

Follow the steps below to complete the slot booking process:

  • Visit the official SSC website at ssc.gov.in.
  • On the homepage, open the Candidate Login section.
  • Enter your registered ID and password to log in.
  • Select your preferred exam date, shift and exam city carefully.
  • Complete OTP verification process.
  • Submit the form and save the confirmation details for future reference.
