SSC GD Posts 2026: Name of Forces

The SSC GD Recruitment is conducted for multiple central forces where selected candidates serve as General Duty staff. All these forces are part of the Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs) or allied security organizations. Allocation to a specific force depends on merit and candidate preferences.

  • Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF)
  • Indo-Tibetan Border Police (ITBP)

SSC GD Exam Date 2026 Admit Card

The commission issues the admit card 2–3 days prior to the exam date. Candidates who have successfully registered can download their SSC GD Constable Admit Card 2026 using their registration number and password from the official website.

SSC GD admit card acts as an entry pass to the examination hall. Candidates must carry a printed copy of the admit card to the exam centre, as entry will not be permitted without it. The hall ticket contains important details such as exam date, shift timing, reporting time, centre address, and instructions.

How to download SSC GD Admit Card at ssc.gov.in

  • Visit the official website at ssc.gov.in
  • On the homepage, go to the admit card section
  • Click on SSC GD Constable admit card download link
  • Enter your login credentials
  • Your admit card will appear on the screen
  • Download it and take a printout for future reference
