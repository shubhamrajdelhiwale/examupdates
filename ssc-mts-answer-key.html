SSC MTS Answer Key 2026 Objection Window

After the provisional SSC MTS Answer Key 2026 is released, the Commission will activate an objection window. SSC MTS 2026 Marking Scheme

To calculate your tentative score using the SSC MTS Answer Key 2026, you must understand the marking scheme properly:

  • Session I: No negative marking.
  • Session II: Negative marking of 1 mark for each incorrect answer.
  • Correct Answer: Each correct response carries 3 marks.

After the release of the SSC MTS Answer Key 2026, the following stages will take place:

  • Final Answer Key & Result: The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) will publish the final answer key after reviewing objections, along with the result and list of shortlisted candidates.
