    SSC MTS Exam Analysis 2026 Today

    The computer-based test started today for Multi-Tasking (Non-Technical) Staff, and Havaldar (CBIC & CBN) Examination 2025, and the 1st shift concluded at 10:30 am. Candidates who have their exams in the upcoming days and shifts must check the difficulty level and good attempts for the 1st shift.

    As SSC has strictly prohibited the sharing of questions for the SSC MTS Exam, we are discussing the good attempts and difficulty level.

    SSC MTS Exam Analysis 2026 (Till Date)

    This year, around 36.17 lakh candidates have applied; therefore, the exam has been scheduled from 4th to 19th February 2026 in multiple shifts each day. There were 4 sections in the exam, namely Numerical & Mathematical Ability, Reasoning Ability & Problem-Solving, General Awareness, and English Language & Comprehension. There were 90 questions for 270 marks, and the duration of the exam was 90 minutes.

    SSC MTS Exam Analysis 2026 Difficulty Level

    The overall difficulty level of the SSC MTS 1st Shift of 4th February exam was of Easy to Moderate level, with English Language & Comprehension being easy. Candidates also reviewed that the questions from General Awareness were comparatively moderate.

    SSC MTS Exam Analysis 2026: Section-Wise Overview

    The detailed subject-wise analysis of the SSC MTS Exam 2026 is provided below:

    ✨ Reasoning Ability & Problem Solving

    The Reasoning section was considered very easy and highly scoring. Questions were straightforward and involved minimal complexity. As a result, most candidates were able to attempt nearly all questions in this section with good accuracy.

    ✨ General English

    The English section was rated very easy by most candidates, enabling many to attempt almost all the questions. A significant number of questions were based on narration and active–passive voice. Only 2–3 questions appeared from antonyms and synonyms, which was comparatively fewer than in previous exams.

    ✨ General Awareness

    In the General Awareness section, current affairs carried higher weightage. Questions were primarily asked from important events of 2024 and 2025. Other topics included folk dances, major sports events such as the Olympics and Youth Games, and recent appointments across various sectors.

    ✨ Numerical & Mathematical Ability

    The Mathematics section was perceived as difficult by a majority of candidates. Most questions were calculation-intensive and lengthy, making this section time-consuming. Many candidates found it to be the most challenging part of the examination.

    SSC MTS Exam Review 2026 – FAQs

    1. What was the overall difficulty level of SSC MTS Today exam?

    The overall difficulty level of the SSC MTS exam was reported as Easy to Moderate. Most candidates found Reasoning and English easy, while General Awareness was moderate and Mathematics was comparatively time-consuming.

    2. Which section was the easiest in today’s SSC MTS exam?

    As per student feedback, the Reasoning Ability & Problem-Solving section and the English Language & Comprehension section were considered the easiest in today’s shift. Many candidates were able to attempt almost all questions in these sections.

    3. Which section was the most difficult or time-consuming?

    Numerical & Mathematical Ability was considered the most time-consuming section. Several questions were calculation-based and lengthy, which required careful time management.

    4. How many questions were asked in SSC MTS 2026 exam?

    The SSC MTS 2026 computer-based exam consisted of 90 questions for a total of 270 marks. The duration of the exam was 90 minutes.

    5. What were the good attempts for SSC MTS 4 February Shift 1?

    The overall good attempts for the 1st shift were around 72–77 questions. Section-wise, candidates reported attempting around 18 questions in Reasoning, 18 in Mathematics, 14–16 in General Awareness, and 20–22 in English.

    6. What type of questions were asked in the General Awareness section?

    The General Awareness section included questions from current affairs of 2024 and 2025, important sports events, recent appointments, government schemes, and major national and international events.

    7. What topics were covered in the English section?

    The English section included questions on narration, active and passive voice, error detection, synonyms, antonyms, and basic grammar concepts. The overall level of this section was considered easy.

    8. What were the exam timings for SSC MTS 4 February 2026?

    The 1st shift was conducted from 9:00 am to 10:30 am. The exam is being conducted in multiple shifts daily as per the official schedule.
