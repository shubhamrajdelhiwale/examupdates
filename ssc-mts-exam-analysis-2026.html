SSC MTS Exam Analysis 2026: Section-Wise Review (1st & 2nd Shift)

The detailed subject-wise SSC MTS Exam Shift 1 & Shift 2 Analysis is given in this article below:

General English

The English section was easy in both shifts. Most candidates were able to attempt almost all questions with good accuracy. A large number of questions were asked from narration and activeâ€“passive voice. Only 2-3 questions came from antonyms and synonyms, which was less compared to previous exams. Overall, English remained a scoring and time-saving section in both shifts.

General Awareness

The GK section had more weightage of current affairs in both shifts. Questions were asked from major events of 2024 and 2025, along with topics like folk dances, Olympics, Youth Games, awards, and music. In Shift 2, some aspirants felt this section was moderate to a little tough, though most questions were direct. A question related to Article 157 of the Constitution was also asked.

Numerical & Mathematical Ability

Maths showed some variation between the two shifts. In Shift 1, maths was considered tough and lengthy, and many students found calculations time consuming. In Shift 2, the level was easy to moderate, but still required good calculation speed. Overall, maths remained the most challenging section for many candidates.

Reasoning Ability & Problem Solving

Reasoning was the easiest part of the paper in both shifts. The questions were direct and did not involve much complexity. In Shift 2, around 3-4 questions were asked from mirror images, while other questions were from common topics like series, analogy, and coding-decoding. Most students attempted nearly all questions in this section.
