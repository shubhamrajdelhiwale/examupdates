SSC Recruitment 2026 LIVE @ssc.gov.in – CGL, JE, Selection Post Notification Latest Updates

By MockTest Adda • 2 days ago

The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) is set to release the official notifications for SSC Recruitment 2026-27 for CGL, JE, and Selection Post vacancies in April 2026. Earlier, it was expected to be released on 31st March. The application process will begin immediately along with the official notification, and will continue until April 2026. However, as per the latest update, there is a delay in the SSC Notifications due to administrative issues. Aspirants should not wait for last-day announcement and complete their One-Time Registration beforehand. It is crucial to keep important documents ready and monitor the official SSC website at ssc.gov.in regularly for any notification related updates.

SSC Recruitment 2026

SSC Recruitment 2026 will be conducted as per the official SSC Calendar 2026–27 with the recruitment cycle starting from April 2026. Major notifications for CGL, JE, and Selection Post are expected in April 2026, followed by CHSL and Stenographer in May.

SSC Recruitment 2026: What Should Candidates Do?

With SSC Recruitment 2026 notifications expected from April, candidates should complete One-Time Registration (OTR) and keep documents ready for application. Aspirants preparing for CGL, JE, and Selection Post must regularly check official updates.

  • Complete SSC OTR in advance to avoid last-day errors.
  • Keep scanned documents ready (photo, signature, certificates).
  • Check eligibility criteria before applying.
  • Apply early to avoid traffic and server issues.
  • Track official updates regularly on SSC website.
