How to Download TGBIE Inter Hall Ticket 2026 via SMS?

Steps to Get TGBIE Inter Hall Ticket on Mobile

  • Open the SMS app on your registered mobile number.
  • Type the message in the prescribed format provided by TGBIE (e.g., TSINTER <Space> Registration Number).
  • Send the SMS to the official number announced by the Telangana Board.
  • Wait for a confirmation reply from TGBIE.
  • You will receive your hall ticket details including exam centre and subject information.
  • Save the message or take a screenshot for future reference.

Important Points While Downloading TS Inter Hall Ticket on Mobile

  • Send the SMS only from the registered mobile number linked with your college/board records.

  • Standard SMS charges may apply as per your mobile operator.
  • If there is any delay, try again later or contact your college authorities.
