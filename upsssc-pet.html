UPSSSC PET 2025 Notification: Online Form, Eligibility, Exam Date & Selection Process

The Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC) is expected to release the UPSSSC PET 2025 notification soon on its official website. The Preliminary Eligibility Test (PET) is conducted as a qualifying examination for various Group C posts under the Uttar Pradesh government. Candidates must complete the online registration process within the specified dates once the notification is officially announced.

What is the UPSSSC PET Exam?

The UPSSSC PET stands for Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission Preliminary Eligibility Test. It is a state-level screening examination conducted for recruitment to various Group ‘C’ posts under the Uttar Pradesh government. The PET serves as the first stage of selection and acts as a qualifying gateway for candidates who wish to appear in the respective Main examinations for different posts.

The full form of UP PET 2025 is Preliminary Eligibility Test. As per eligibility criteria, candidates must have passed at least Class 10 (High School) from a recognised board to apply. The examination consists of objective-type multiple-choice questions covering subjects such as General Studies, General Hindi, Numerical Ability, and Reasoning Ability. The total marks for the PET exam are 200, and candidates must secure a minimum of 33% marks to qualify.

The duration of the UPSSSC PET exam is 2 hours. Once qualified, candidates can apply for various post-specific Main examinations conducted by UPSSSC. The registration process is conducted online through the official UPSSSC website. Importantly, the PET scorecard remains valid for a period of three years from the date of result declaration, allowing candidates multiple opportunities to apply for different recruitments during this validity period.

UPSSSC PET Online Form 2025 – Complete Application Process

Interested candidates can apply online for UPSSSC PET 2025 through the official website once the notification is released. The last date to submit the online application form is June 17, 2025. Applicants are advised to carefully fill out the form and follow the official instructions to avoid errors during submission.

Step 1: Visit the Official Website

Access the UPSSSC Candidate Registration Portal through the official website. On the homepage, locate the advertisement related to UPSSSC PET 2025. Click on the “Apply Online” option and then select the “Candidate Registration” button to begin the process.

Step 2: Online Registration

After opening the application page, find the relevant advertisement number and click on the “Apply” button displayed against it. Carefully read the instructions mentioned on the page before proceeding with the registration. Complete the basic registration details to generate your login credentials.

Step 3: Fill the UP PET Online Form

Log in using your credentials and fill in all required personal, academic, and contact details accurately. Upload scanned copies of your photograph, signature, and other required documents as per the prescribed format (JPEG/JPG, maximum 30 KB for photograph). Ensure all details are correct before moving to the payment stage.

Step 4: Application Fee Payment

Pay the applicable examination fee according to your category using debit card, credit card, internet banking, or SBI e-challan. After successful payment, review the form and submit it. Candidates should download and print the final submitted application form for future reference.

PET Exam 2025 Selection Process

The UP PET is a preliminary screening examination conducted for recruitment to various Group C posts under the Uttar Pradesh Government. Candidates applying for the UP PET 2025 will have to undergo multiple stages as part of the selection process for the respective post.

Stage 1: Preliminary Eligibility Test (PET)

This is the first stage where a large number of candidates appear to test their understanding of General Studies, General Hindi, Numerical Ability, and Reasoning through an objective-type examination. Only candidates who qualify in the PET will be eligible to proceed to the next stage.

Stage 2: Main Written Examination

Candidates who clear document scrutiny and meet the required cutoff will be called for the Main examination. This stage is conducted to assess candidates’ subject knowledge and job-specific proficiency.

Stage 3: Skill Test / Interview

Depending on the nature of the post, candidates may be required to appear for a skill test, physical test, or interview. Final selection is based on overall performance across all stages, followed by document verification and preparation of a category-wise merit list.

UPSSSC PET Exam Eligibility 2025

Candidates applying for the UPSSSC PET 2025 must fulfill the eligibility criteria specified in the official notification. Applicants must have passed High School (Class 10) or an equivalent examination from a recognized board. The age limit generally falls between 18 and 40 years, with relaxation provided to reserved categories as per government norms.

UPSSSC PET Exam Pattern 2025

As per the official exam pattern, the Preliminary Eligibility Test (PET) is conducted for a total of 100 marks. The difficulty level of questions is generally based on the NCERT syllabus from Class 10 to Class 12. The examination consists of objective-type multiple-choice questions, and candidates are advised to understand the complete exam pattern to prepare effectively.

UPSSSC PET Exam 2025 FAQs

What are the total UPSSSC PET vacancies released?

The UPSSSC PET itself is a qualifying examination and does not specify fixed vacancies. The number of vacancies is announced separately for each Group C recruitment notification released after the PET results.

What is the UPSSSC PET Application Process?

Candidates must apply online through the official UPSSSC website. The process includes registration, filling out personal and academic details, uploading required documents, paying the application fee, and submitting the form.

How much is the UPSSSC PET exam application fee?

The application fee varies by category. Generally, General and OBC candidates pay Rs. 185, SC/ST candidates pay Rs. 95, and PwD candidates pay Rs. 25.

What will be the PET Exam selection process?

The selection process begins with the Preliminary Eligibility Test (PET). Qualified candidates can then apply for post-specific Main examinations, followed by skill tests or interviews depending on the recruitment rules.

What is the age limit to apply for the UPSSSC PET exam?

The general age limit is between 18 and 40 years. Age relaxation is provided to reserved categories as per Uttar Pradesh government norms.

Who is eligible for UPSSSC PET Registration 2025?

Candidates who have passed at least Class 10 (High School) from a recognized board are eligible to apply for the UPSSSC PET 2025 examination.

Is there any negative marking in UP PET 2025?

Yes, as per previous exam patterns, negative marking is applicable in the PET examination. Candidates are advised to check the official notification for exact details.

Is UPSSSC PET Notification 2025 out?

What is the last date to apply for the UP PET 2025 vacancy?

The last date to apply will be mentioned in the official notification. Candidates must complete the online application before the specified deadline.

What is the validity of UPSSSC PET 2025 scorecard?
